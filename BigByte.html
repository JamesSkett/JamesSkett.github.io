BIG-BYTE

BIG-BYTE is a small mobile game and my mate developed using unity and released onto the Google Play Store. The aim of the game is to pick up good objects to increase your health, when your health reaches the top of the bar you level up and your size increases but so does the size of everything else around you. So eventually you get huge and the game gets harder.

It uses the Google Play services, so we could get global high scores and achievements for the game, there is also an option of local high scores that are stored on the phone.

Link to download: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.LilNibble.BigByte

One Way Out

One Way Out was made for one of my second year university assignments. The assignment involved creating a micro design document, an art style guide and a working prototype of the game with test iterations.

One way out is a top down shooter where the objective is to get to the exit of the maze without dying. To get to the exit you will need to find the key to open the door, the keys will be hidden at random areas of the maze you must work your way round the maze to find it. Enemies will try to kill you but you can kill them but you have to be careful as you may run out of ammo. There are lives dotted around the maze but if you run out of lives you die and the game resets.

The idea for the game came from an old project that I completed in college, the game plays in pretty much the same way but with some new mechanics, pickups and a different level design.
